Jam with Friends

The premises of this project stemmed from how I feel people should learn how to collaborate. Most of the music we listened to stemmed from collaborative efforts. So the application I built is geared towards college students looking to jam with other colleagues of similar tastes and musical prefences and integrate them into the new city they moved to.

    How it works


  • You sign up and build a profile
  • From there you can rent out a rehersal space. And your friends/other registered users can sign up for the rehersal time.
  • The goal is to link the community with the students. Give Local shops and venues the opportunity to market themselves to these new incoming students.
